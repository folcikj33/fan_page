Dave Matthews Band Fan Site!

The Dave Matthews Band (DMB for short) is a super cool American rock jam-band who formed in
1991 to show the world what real music sounds like. Some people like to give me guff about
my love for the DMB, claiming that the songs are too long, too boring, and that the lead singer
sounds like some sort of bohemian orca whale swallowing its own tongue as it whales off-key. But
all of my friends are wrong.

This here below is what genius looks like, people.
